Frequently Asked Questions

We collaborate closely with your team, combining our expertise with your valuable insights.

What do I need to apply?
  • FICO credit score of 480+
  • 3+ months in business
  • $5,000+ gross average monthly revenue over the past 3 months
What collateral do I need?

Our products are revenue-based. So no collateral is needed. This gives us more flexibility when sourcing products for you from conventional banks.

What is the rate?

Rates vary based on your business's specific needs and revenue profile, typically ranging from 5% to 25%. For a precise quote tailored to your situation, please apply or reach out to our team.

How fast can I get funding?

We work with a top-tier network, with different processes and products. So there’s no one answer.
We can say that most funding options can be in your bank account within 24 hours.

How long does the application take?

Around 5 minutes.

What impact does applying have on my credit score?

There’s zero impact on your credit score.
That’s because we do one soft pull to verify eligibility. See terms and conditions below.

What is a soft pull?

A soft pull is where we look at how well you manage credit. It is the same type of pull you get when Experian/Credit Karma checks your credit profile.
This will not show up on your report or impact it in any way.

Will I speak to a person about my funding?

Every successful application gets assigned a dedicated funding manager.
They’ll work with you on a personalized 1-2-1 basis.
